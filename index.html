Questions?

Frequently asked

Do I need technical knowledge to set it up? +
No. StockFlow is designed to be running in under 30 minutes. No IT consultant, no training courses, no implementation project. If you can use a browser, you can use StockFlow.
Can I import my existing products from Excel? +
Yes. You can import your existing product list via CSV upload. It takes about 5 minutes to migrate your entire catalogue.
How is this different from AFAS? +
AFAS is a full ERP system designed for medium to large companies with IT departments. StockFlow is built specifically for 1–20 person businesses who need inventory management, invoicing, and CRM — without the complexity or the €400+/month price tag.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. All data is stored securely in the cloud, encrypted at rest and in transit. We use Supabase infrastructure which is SOC 2 certified. Your data is never shared with third parties.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. StockFlow is fully mobile-responsive and works great on phones and tablets. You can even install it as an app on your home screen (no App Store required).
